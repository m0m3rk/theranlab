How to Convert Watts to dBm

dBm is a logarithmic scale relative to 1 milliwatt. To convert from linear watts, take the base-10 log of the wattage, multiply by 10, and add 30.

Watts to dBm — Formula
P(dBm) = 10 × log₁₀(P(W) / 1W) + 30
The +30 shifts the reference from dBW (relative to 1 W) to dBm (relative to 1 mW = 0.001 W).
Example — Convert 20 watts to dBm
P(dBm) = 10 × log₁₀(20) + 30
P(dBm) = 10 × 1.3010 + 30
P(dBm) = 13.01 + 30
P(dBm) = 43.01 dBm
Quick Rules to Remember
• 0.001 W (1 mW) = 0 dBm (the reference)
• Doubling power adds ~3 dBm
• 10× power adds exactly 10 dBm
• 1 W = 30 dBm  ·  10 W = 40 dBm  ·  100 W = 50 dBm
